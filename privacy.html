Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 5, 2026

Welcome to Rosternado. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the App. This includes:

  • Account Data: Name, Email address, and authentication identifiers from Google or Apple ID.
  • Roster Data: Professional flight schedules or duty rosters that you upload or sync to the App.

2. Social Features & Roster Sharing

Friend Roster Viewing: Rosternado allows you to connect with friends. By connecting, you agree to share your monthly schedule visibility with your confirmed friends. You can manage your connections and visibility settings directly within the app's settings.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide a seamless experience, we use the following third-party service providers:

  • Supabase: Used as our primary database to securely store your profile and roster information.
  • Firebase: Used specifically for sending push notifications regarding schedule updates or friend requests.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions and verify purchase status.

4. Data Deletion & Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services. You have the right to delete your data at any time.

Rosternado provides a dedicated "Delete Account" option within the app settings. Once triggered, this will remove your profile, authentication links, and all stored roster data from our primary databases.

5. Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, please remember that no electronic transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.
