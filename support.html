Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my roster?
Pull down on the roster screen. Your airline's roster website opens in-app — sign in and tap Save to import your duties.
Who can see my roster?
Only friends you have accepted. Rosters are private by default and never appear in search.
My airline isn't supported for upload.
You can still add duties manually. Email us your airline — we add parsers over time.
How do I delete my account?
Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, roster, and friendships.
How do I manage my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your App Store account settings on your device.
