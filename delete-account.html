Delete Your Rosternado Account

We are sorry to see you go. Below you will find the instructions and details regarding the deletion of your account and associated data.

What data will be deleted:
  • Your personal profile (Name, Email, Profile Picture).
  • All uploaded Roster and Schedule data.
  • Your friend connections and sharing permissions.
  • Notification tokens stored in Firebase.

Option 1: Delete via the App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your data is directly within the Rosternado app:

  1. Open the Rosternado app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap on Account.
  4. Select Delete Account and confirm.

Option 2: Delete via Web Request

If you have already uninstalled the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us from the email address associated with your account.

Request Deletion via Email

Data Retention Policy

Once your request is processed, your data is permanently removed from our Supabase databases. Please note that subscription billing history managed by RevenueCat may be retained for accounting and legal compliance purposes as required by tax laws.
