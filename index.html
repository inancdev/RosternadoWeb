ROSTERNADO
Flight roster sharing for crew

Rosternado keeps your flight schedule in one place — import it from your airline, see every duty at a glance, and share it with the people who want to know where you are.

Coming soon to the App Store & Google Play

What it does

Import your roster

Open your airline's roster website inside the app and import your duties in one tap. Supported airlines are added over time.

Every duty, clearly

Flights, standby, training, days off and leave — shown in a clean chronological list, with multi-leg flights grouped together.

Share with friends

Send a friend request and, once accepted, your friends and family can follow your upcoming flights. Private by default.

Built for UTC

All times handled in UTC with airline-grade timezone accuracy. View duties in UTC, device time, or your base time.

Works offline

Your roster is cached on your device, so it's there even with no signal — mid-flight or far from a tower.

Add duties manually

No parser for your airline yet? Add and edit any duty by hand, including multi-leg flights.

Who it's for

Aviation crew

Pilots and cabin crew — keep your roster organised, track the flight you're on, and let loved ones see your schedule.

Friends & family

Not crew? Follow one crew member's roster and always know when they're flying, landing, or finally home.
